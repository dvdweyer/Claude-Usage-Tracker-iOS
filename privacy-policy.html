Privacy Policy

Claude Usage Tracker  ·  Last updated: 3 June 2026

The short version: Claude Usage Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server controlled by the developer. Everything stays on your device.

What this app does

Claude Usage Tracker reads your Claude AI usage statistics (session and weekly token consumption) and displays them on your iPhone and in a home screen widget. It does this by contacting Anthropic's servers directly from your device using your own Claude session key.

Data we do not collect

The developer has no server, analytics service, or data pipeline. The following are never transmitted to the developer or any third party:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your Claude session key or organisation ID
  • Your usage statistics or token counts
  • Your device identifier, IP address, or location
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any usage or behavioural analytics

The app contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising frameworks, or crash-reporting tools.

Data stored on your device

All data the app stores remains exclusively on your device:

  • Session key — stored in the iOS Keychain with the WhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly protection class. It is never written to iCloud or included in unencrypted backups.
  • Profile settings (profile names, organisation ID, refresh interval) — stored in app-local UserDefaults.
  • Widget cache — the most recent usage snapshot is written to an App Group UserDefaults container so the home screen widget can read it without launching the app.

Outbound network requests

The app makes HTTPS requests only to Anthropic's own servers on your behalf:

  • claude.ai/api — to retrieve your usage statistics
  • console.anthropic.com/api — to retrieve optional overage and credit-grant data

These requests are authenticated with your session key and go directly from your device to Anthropic. The developer never sees, intercepts, or logs these requests or their responses. Anthropic's own Privacy Policy governs how they handle your data.

Notifications

If you opt in, the app sends local notifications when your usage crosses configurable thresholds (75 %, 90 %, 95 %). These are generated entirely on your device — no push-notification service is used and no data leaves your device to produce them.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy
